Keyboard

/'ki:b:d/

noun

Meaning

  • (etc.) A set keys used to operate a typewriter, computer etc.
  • A component of many instruments including the piano, organ and harpischord consisting of usually black and white keys that cause different tones to be produced when struck.
  • A device with keys of a musical keyboard, used to control electronic sound-producing devices which may be built into or seprate from the keyboard device.
